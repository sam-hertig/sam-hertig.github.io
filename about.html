Welcome

Our passion is to bring scientific data to life by creating engaging visualizations for a wide range of audiences. A broad scientific background, a good eye for graphics and extensive teaching experience allows us to illustrate complex concepts and make them easily accessible to both specialists and the general public. We have created a wide range of visual content, including infographics, conference posters, scientific figures, and animations for university outreach projects and publications in high-impact, peer-reviewed scientific journals with an interdisciplinary readership. We also develop interactive and 3D content for the web, and can assist you with data analysis and data visualization projects on a consulting basis. Furthermore, we are available for workshops and tutorials on scientific visualization.

We strongly believe that visualization is key to insight and understanding, but technical expertise is not enough: Solutions developed by Hertig Visualizations combine cutting edge technology and software with the quality and accuracy of Swiss design and the clarity and elegance of Japanese aesthetics. We build bridges that connect minds, scientifically as well as culturally. We look forward to discussing your next visualization project with you.
